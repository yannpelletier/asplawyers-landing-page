## Why Markdown?

Markdown is a lightweight markup language that's perfect for writing blog posts, documentation, or notes. Here’s why I love it:

- **Simple Syntax**: Easy to learn and write.
- **Portable**: Works across platforms and tools.
- **Clean Output**: Converts to beautiful HTML with minimal effort.
- **Flexible**: Supports text, images, code, and more.
